Adobe XD to Arma 3

Create your GUIs in Adobe XD, export them as SVGs, use them in Arma 3

    Limitations
    • Adobe XD to Arma 3 (XD2A3 hereafter) will not accept Artboards format other than 1920x1080
    • XD2A3 will not export elements other than Rectangles, Texts, Images or Groups
    • XD2A3 will not export your GUI if you have borders on any of your element

    Adobe XD - Export settings
    • Format: SVG
    • Styling: Presentation Attributes
    • Save images: Link
    • File size: Do not minimize
